  • Cool Facts About Waco
  • Dr Pepper was created one year before Coca-Cola. So Waco, Texas holds a unique spot in soda history as the birthplace of America's original soft drink.
  • The Waco Mammoth National Monument is a rare paleontological site where the remains of a nursery herd of Ice Age Colombian mammoths were discovered.
  • Waco is home to Baylor University, a major private Christian university founded in 1845, making it the oldest continually operating university in Texas.
  • The historic Waco Suspension Bridge, built in 1870, was the first bridge to span the Brazos River, and was a key part for cattle drives on the Chisholm Trail. It's now a pedestrian-only landmark and popular for photos. It was the first major suspension bridge in Texas. The engineers received help from the company that built the Brooklyn Bridge. Longest Suspension bridge west of the Mississippi (at the time) at a 475-foot span.
